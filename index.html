Project

As a developer, I always want my customers to have a good quality game and have a great time when playing games but not being addicted.Therefore, I suppose that an offline mobile game would be a great idea. And the game's name is The angry den and it will available on both IOS or Android systems for free.

The mobile game has become a leisure activity in many years and people might not deny the benefit of playing games. Moreover, according to IndianExpress, ”the game has already crossed 200 million downloads and has at least 30 million active users. So, the popularity of games on mobile devices is steadily increasing and with that, the demand for excellent graphics as well.” which means the game industry can be a huge market in the next few years. Hence, a start-up with a mobile game could be a potential way for my career.

This angry hen probably will be a role-playing game. During the game, the players will role as hens and their eggs are threatened by wolves. Therefore, you have to go to the jungle, then try to find and defeat the wolf's den, and regain your eggs. On your journey, you will come across a lot of villages such as pig’s village and monkey’s village, and you will have new friends who role as supporters. The game has five chapters which represent five villages. In each village, you will have small missions to gain golds and experiences. The similarity of five villages is that villagers all hate the Wolves because they have to pay tribute to them, if not do, the wolf will destroy the village. So, at the end of each chapter, you and your allies will fight with a wolf and each wolf will give you a clue to the wolf's den. Additionally, in each village, you will have a chance to buy or upgrade your weapons such as swords or shields. Interestingly, you will also be able to learn special skills which will increase your damage. The angry hen is an offline game, so players do not need to worry about being disrupted by advertising. Moreover, all the missions are based on an attractive plot so players do not have to do some extra missions such as daily login gifts. Instead of that, this game has some mini-games at the main interface in case players have completed the story.

About tools, I might use the Unreal Engine which is the next version of the Unreal Development Kit in order to design my mobile game. Simply because it is suitable for junior developer like me due to the shortage of prior experiences. Moreover, it has many features with supporting me in making a mobile game as well as in PCs and Game consoles.Besides, Unreal Engine is used commonly by many game developers.

Additionally, I found out that unity also can be a great tool to start my first game app. According to blog.eduonix.com,”One of the highly famed choices for creating games, Unity allows the user to create 2D and 3D games. It is a favorite of the developer community to create an abundant amount of AAA-class games.” Moreover, on Unity web pages, there are plenty of tutorials that can help me understand Unity’s applications insight.

Personally, I have to prepare myself a standard knowledge about some programming languages such as C++, Java, HTML5 or PHP. On the other hand, about non-technical skills, I am a junior developer, so it might take a lot of time to create and run my game. From that point, I will work with others in order to complete faster and gain more experience. More importantly, I need to work consistently with the design team because I need to make sure that my game content is not misleading or irrelevant to my original story.

There are also many problems before I start this project. Firstly, at the moment, the main players prefer to play online games since it is more fun, especially when you play with your friends. Another point is that The angry hen is my first app, consequently, I need to purchase some money for advertising. And of course, I have to find investment from businesses or might be my parent.

If this game is successful, I will make more version of The angry hen. And this time, it will be an online version, I might use Facebook or Instagram in order to help players make new friends. Additionally, I can gain an amount of money from this game and I can make more games to increase my income. In contrast, if the angry hen is not popular, I will lose a small amount of money but I will learn new things from my fail. For example, using programming languages more effectively, so my game has more features more attractive or learning to use marketing strategies to advertise my game better.
